About

Card Knight is a real-time action deckbuilder — no waiting your turn. You run, dodge, and fire off cards live across a grid battlefield, in a handcrafted single-player RPG. Play Oden, a young adventurer who stumbles into a destiny written in cards on his first day at work, and travel a lush HD-2D world: quiet shrine-towns, dark forests, lantern-lit harbors, and the dust-blown Rustmarch — learning the secrets of the Old Card Knight Order along the way.

Key Features

  • Real-time card combat — reflexes and deckcraft, not menus and turns.
  • A handcrafted HD-2D world — every tile placed by hand.
  • Build your deck — collect, craft, and customize; discover synergies that turn the tide.
  • A story, voiced — a cast of characters with fully voiced dialogue across a story-driven campaign.
  • Native Steam Deck support — built and tested on the Deck.
