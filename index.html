Not turn‑based. Real‑time.

Forget waiting your turn. You run, dodge, and fire off cards live across a grid battlefield — a love letter to Mega Man Battle Network, built as a handcrafted single‑player RPG. It's a deckbuilder for people who want their hands on the controls, not a roguelike.

Action Deck Combat

Sling spells and steel in real time. Position matters, timing matters, and your deck is your arsenal.

A Handcrafted World

Explore towns, forests, harbors and rune‑lit ruins — every tile placed by hand in lush HD‑2D pixel art.

Build Your Legend

Collect, craft and customize a deck that fits your style. Discover synergies that turn the tide.

A Story, Voiced

Meet a cast of characters with voiced, fully‑editable dialogue across a story‑driven campaign.

A world worth wandering

From the quiet shrines of Cardhollow to storm‑lashed crests and the dust‑blown Rustmarch, the world of Card Knight is built to be explored — and it remembers you.

Meet Oden

A young adventurer who stumbles into a destiny written in cards. Guide him from a humble shrine‑town to the heart of an unraveling kingdom.
